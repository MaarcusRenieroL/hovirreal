About

Hovirreal aims to simulate physical classrooms into an online space using virtual reality simulation. With this idea, our objective is to hope to solve two major problems in the present education system.

To bridge the gap in communication between students and teachers by providing a virtual interactive platform.

To make learning fun and effective again

We are a team of college students from a reputed college in Chennai, Tamilnadu, India. This business idea was an outcome of our brainstorming sessions while contemplating the flaws in the current online way of education we had during the pandemic. We wanted to come up with a solution to overcome the difficulties faced by the students in the online education system and to improve the quality and delivery of education.

Overall improving the teaching-learning process.

FEATURES

VR CLASSROOM ENVIRONMENT

We aim to create a physical classroom like environment onto a digital space using Virtual Reality enabling the users to get the feel of a classroom and end the monotonous feel of the current meet based online education system.

HOLOGRAM MODELS

3D models tightly integrated within the VR environment that will enable teachers to teach better by showing live 3D examples of concepts they teach and enabling students to understand the concepts better while sparking their creativity at the same time

CLASSROOM CUSTOMIZABILITY

Change the overall environment of the digitally created classroom to suite the theme of the class and keep the environment fresh and exciting at all times
